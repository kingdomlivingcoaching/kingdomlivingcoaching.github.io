Meet Your Coach

Braxton Bonds

Christian Life Coach, Mentor & Spiritual Director

Braxton Bonds is a Christian life coach, mentor, and spiritual director whose passion is helping people walk closely with Jesus in the middle of their real, everyday lives.

For years, Braxton has worked with athletes, students, families, and leaders ‚Äî guiding them through questions of identity, purpose, discipline, relationships, and faith. His heart is not just to encourage people, but to help them experience deep, lasting transformation through intentional spiritual growth and practical life change.

Braxton believes many people sincerely love God but feel stuck when it comes to actually living out their faith. Through coaching and spiritual direction, he walks alongside individuals to help them gain clarity, build healthy rhythms, strengthen marriages and relationships, and pursue the calling God has placed on their lives.

His approach is both deeply spiritual and highly practical ‚Äî combining biblical wisdom, honest conversation, goal-setting, accountability, and discipleship. Sessions are designed to help people hear from God, grow in discipline, and move forward with courage and direction.

Braxton is a husband, father, coach, and educator who has spent years mentoring and discipling people in both athletic and spiritual settings. His life and work are rooted in a desire to see people fully alive in Christ and faithfully living the purpose they were created for.
